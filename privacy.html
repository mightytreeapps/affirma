Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 May 2026

Affirma values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1) Information we collect

  • Account information: your email address and password, used to create and secure your account.
  • App content: the affirmations you save or write yourself, the voices you add to your library, and your daily streak and practice progress.

2) How we use information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To provide core app functionality.
  • To communicate important service updates and security notices.

3) Sharing of information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share limited data with trusted service providers (for example authentication, hosting) only to operate the app.

4) Data retention

  • We retain information only as long as needed for legitimate business and legal purposes.
  • You may request account deletion, after which we will remove or anonymize data as required, unless we must retain certain records by law.

5) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information.

6) Your choices and rights

  • You can update account details where available in the app.
  • You can request deletion of your account data through the app.
  • Depending on your location, you may have additional privacy rights under local law.

7) Policy updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated in-app or through other appropriate channels.

8) Contact

Privacy questions or requests can be sent to the support contact listed in the app store listing or official support channel.
