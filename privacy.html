Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026

Questions? Email us at mightytreeapps@gmail.com.

The short version

  • We only collect what we need to make the app work for you.
  • We don't sell your data.
  • You can delete your account and everything tied to it at any time, from inside the app.

What we collect

When you sign up

Your email address and password, so we can create your account and let you sign in. Passwords are stored securely and we never see them in plain text.

When you use the app

  • The affirmations you save or write yourself.
  • The voices you add to your library
  • Your daily streak and practice progress.

On your device only

Some things never leave your phone — your onboarding answers (what you'd like the app to focus on) and your reminder times.

When you subscribe

If you buy a subscription, the App Store handles the payment. We're simply told whether your subscription is active so we can unlock paid features. We don't see or store your card details.

How we use your information

  • To create and protect your account.
  • To save your affirmations, voices, and progress so they're there next time you open the app.
  • To unlock paid features for active subscribers.
  • To respond when you write to us.

We don't share your affirmations or data with anyone else.

Who we work with

To run Affirma we rely on a small number of trusted service providers — for example, to host your data securely, handle App Store subscriptions, and generate spoken affirmations. Each only sees the information it needs to do its job.

Keeping or deleting your data

We keep your account and the things tied to it for as long as your account is active. To delete everything, open the app, go to the Account screen, and tap Delete account. Your affirmations, voices, streak, and account record are all removed.

You can also email mightytreeapps@gmail.com and we'll take care of it for you.

Security

We protect your information with industry-standard safeguards and we take keeping your data safe seriously.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be here.
